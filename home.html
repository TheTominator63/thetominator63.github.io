Stirling Youth Folk Club

ABOUT THE CLUB

We are a group of Young Musicians in the Stirling Area that are interested in playing and learning traditional music together, and getting more people involved in Scottish folk music.

WATCH US PLAY

We play every other Monday Night at Nicky-Tam’s Bar and Bothy in Stirling, 9-11pm. Come along for some tunes!​

LEARN MORE

If you’re interested in how the club came to be, check out the History page. If you want to see photos or videos of the club, have a look at the Gallery or Videos pages. If you play an instrument and want to learn our tunes, look through our Archive.
